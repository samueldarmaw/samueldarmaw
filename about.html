Bio

I’m Samuel Sutantra Darmawangsa, a UI/UX and graphic designer who obsesses over one thing: pacing. Ever since I was a kid, I’ve paid attention to how movies guide your attention—when to slow down, when to reveal, and how small transitions change what you feel. That same thinking shapes how I design digital products: clear hierarchy, intentional motion, and flows that never feel confusing or heavy. I like building interfaces that feel “invisible”—where the next step is obvious, the feedback is immediate, and the experience has rhythm. I work across Figma and Adobe tools, and I’m comfortable translating design into real UI when needed—because I care about what ships, not just what looks good in a file. Right now I’m looking for roles where I can design end-to-end experiences, strengthen design systems, and turn complex ideas into simple, satisfying interactions.
